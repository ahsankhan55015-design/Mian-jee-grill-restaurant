Our Story

The Taste of
Pure Desi Grill

Nestled in the heart of Al Quoz Industrial, Mian Jee Grill Restaurant has been serving the community of Dubai with the most authentic and flavourful grilled delicacies straight from the charcoal.

From succulent seekh kebabs to perfectly marinated tikkas, every dish is crafted with hand-picked spices and cooked the traditional way — giving you that unforgettable smoky, homestyle flavour.

We believe great food brings people together. Come hungry, leave happy.
